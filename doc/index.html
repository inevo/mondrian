Mondrian

Mondrian is an OLAP engine written in Java.

Read the overview, installation instructions, javadoc, and the roadmap.

The project is named after the artist Piet Mondrian, whose pictures look kind of like how cubes seem in my brain. Which is kind of worrying:

Everything in his life was reasoned or calculated. He was a compulsive neurotic and could never bear to see anything disordered or untidy. He seemed to suffer acutely, for instance, if a table had not been laid with perfect symmetry.

— Hannah Höch on Piet Mondrian, quoted by Edouard Roditi

The source code (including this page) is hosted in John Sichi's perforce server. Thanks to John, and to Perforce for letting us use their server gratis.
